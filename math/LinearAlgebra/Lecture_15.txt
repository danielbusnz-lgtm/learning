The example above shows two important things about the Gram-Schmidt process. Firstly, it can
produce some square roots, so don’t be scared of getting your hands a bit dirty with the compu-
tation, because the ﬁnal answer might turn out to be reasonably nice. And secondly, there exist
many possible choices of orthonormal bases of a vector space, e.g. just compare the vectors we
guessed in (126) with those produced by the example above.
